Library

A library is a collection of resources, typically in the form of books, manuscripts, journals, multimedia materials, and digital content, organized for research, education, or public use. Libraries serve as valuable repositories of knowledge and information, and they play a significant role in society by facilitating learning, research, and access to a wide range of materials.

Libraries are essential institutions that promote literacy, education, and lifelong learning. They provide a wealth of resources and services to individuals and communities, contributing to knowledge dissemination and cultural enrichment. The role of libraries has evolved with the digital age, ensuring that they continue to meet the changing needs of their users.
